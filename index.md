## SUTAGAR

I am the main developer of the Python package SUTAGAR (name). This package is focused on correcting the subhalo finding process of mainstream halo finders during the interactions with their biggest hosts. This algorithm follows the particles of the satellite halos until their internal disruption. The paper detailing the algorithm is available in [arxiv](404). The GitHub repository of this algorithm is [GitHub](404).
